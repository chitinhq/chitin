## Equity sanity check

For frontier-AI offers, **equity dominates cash**. Anthropic example: one engineer reported $4-5M vested in year 1 against a $61.5B valuation. That makes the $443K cash median misleading — true year-1 comp can be $1M+ if equity vesting hits in a strong valuation cycle.

**Framework implication:** Don't anchor on cash-only TC at frontier labs. Ask recruiter for the equity grant + most-recent 409A AND model what it's worth assuming 0%, 50%, 200% appreciation over 4yr vest.

## Verbal questions to ask every recruiter (LOCKED from framework)

For every company in Tier 1 or 2:
- "What's the base / RSU / on-target bonus split?"
- "What level is this role mapped to internally?"
- "What's the current 409A valuation?"
- "How many production deployments did the last 3 FDE hires own end-to-end?"
- "How does FDE field signal feed into the product roadmap?"
- "What's typical travel %?"
- "How many accounts does an FDE own concurrently?"
